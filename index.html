About Me

Hi👋,

I’m Carley! With 4+ years of experience in fitness coaching, I specialise in helping clients achieve their goals sustainably and confidently. I hold certifications in Strength & Conditioning, Nutrition Coaching, and Pre/Postnatal Training.


I work with individuals in Milton Keynes and Hereford, offering tailored fitness programmes that fit your lifestyle, whether you're a busy professional 💼, a new parent 🤰, or just beginning your fitness journey 💪. Together, we’ll build a plan that prioritises your goals, health, and well-being.


Beyond coaching, I’m also a competitive powerlifter, and in 2024, I placed second in the British Showdown Championships. Strength training has transformed my own journey, and I’m passionate about helping others discover the same empowerment through fitness.


Key Values:


  • ✅ Sustainability Over Quick Fixes

    • Fitness should fit into your life—not take over it. I focus on long-term, realistic strategies that help you build strength and confidence without extreme restrictions or burnout. Whether your goal is lifting heavier, feeling stronger, or simply moving better, we’ll create a plan that works for you.


  • ✅ Training That’s Tailored to You

    • No two clients are the same, and neither are their fitness journeys. Your programme will be built around your goals, lifestyle, and preferences, ensuring you see progress in a way that feels enjoyable and rewarding.


  • ✅ Empowerment Through Strength

    • Strength training is about more than just lifting weights—it’s about unlocking your full potential. As a competitive powerlifter, I know firsthand how strength can transform both body and mind. I want to help you feel strong, capable, and confident in every aspect of life.


  • ✅ A Supportive and Inclusive Space

    • Fitness should be a positive experience. Whether you’re stepping into the gym for the first time or working towards a competition, you’ll always have my full support. I’m here to guide, motivate, and celebrate every win—big or small.


Looking forward to helping you achieve your goals! 💪🔥

Services

🏋️ 1:1 Personal Training

Strength & conditioning sessions designed to help you build muscle, improve endurance, and stay motivated. Every session is tailored to your goals, fitness level, and lifestyle.

💻 Online Coaching

Get a fully customised training plan, ongoing support, and accountability – all from the comfort of your home or gym. Includes weekly check-ins and adjustments to keep you progressing.

🥗 Nutrition Coaching

Bespoke meal plans and expert nutrition advice to fuel your goals. Includes personalised meal guides, recipes, and tailored strategies for sustainable eating habits.

🤰 Pre/Postnatal Training

Specialised training for pregnancy and postpartum recovery, focusing on strength, mobility, and confidence. Safe, expert-led workouts to help you feel your best during every stage of motherhood.

📊 Progress Tracking

Stay accountable and track your journey with the Body Dashboard Report – a data-driven progress tool that helps you measure strength gains, body composition, and key milestones.
